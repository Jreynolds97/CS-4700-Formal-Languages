For academic integrity reasons, I have not included the full project description
for this project.

The general purpose of the project was to create a Deterministic Finite Automaton based 
lexer for the Jack language, provided in "The Elements of Computing Systems" by Nisan and Schocken, MIT Press.
For reasons involved in the course, there were further breakdowns of the tokens over those in the book

This has resulted in an incredibly large transition table as defined in transition.py
